Gerai Pi Indonesia - P2App Marketplace

The era of digitalization cannot be stopped, where the existence of digitalized transactions will dominate slowly. Previously, we had adapted it for cross-border transactions supported by the Internet where the existing marketplaces provide a level of Trust for Transactions.

Gerai Pi Indonesia is one of the P2App Marketplaces which will become a Digitalization Transaction Bridge driven by Pi Coin as Crypto Currency by prioritizing Trust/Trust in the Indonesian Pi/Pioneer Community.

Vision:

Become the number 1 and most trusted P2App Utility Marketplace for products and services for the Pi Network community in the world, especially Indonesia.

Mission:

  • Playing the Pi ecosystem directly by involving 3rd parties to ensure security, comfort and trust in transactions.
  • Seeking to embrace all Vendors providing Products/Services to barter with Coin Pi, especially Pioneer Indonesia.
  • Striving to facilitate security, comfort and trust in transactions without Pioneer needing to worry about the security of Pi Coins that have been bartered/transferred for a number of products.
  • All transactions through the Gerai Pi Indonesia Marketplace only use Coin Pi as a medium or a type of exchange/barter tool supported by Coin Pi as Cryptocurrency for Products and services without involving Fiat Money when transactions occur.

The Pi outlet involves several teams who will be ready to serve 24-hour online transactions through the combined support team.
